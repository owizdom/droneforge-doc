## What this means for you

### Using the firmware

The ELRS and Betaflight forks are GPL-3.0. You can:

- Build them.
- Flash them on your hardware.
- Modify them.
- Redistribute the binaries, *as long as you publish your modifications under GPL-3.0 too*.

You **cannot** ship a closed-source product based on these forks without complying with GPL-3.0's source-availability terms.

### Using LeRobot

LeRobot is Apache-2.0. Modify, redistribute, integrate into commercial products, with attribution. No copyleft.

### Using the flasher

`firmware-flasher` is mostly first-party and per-file licensed. If you're embedding the flasher in another product, check each file's header for the specific license. The vendored `esptool` is Apache-2.0.
